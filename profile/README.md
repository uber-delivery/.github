# Uber Delivery - Rides, Delivery, Driver Tools, and Everyday Mobility

## Fast Product Notes

What is Uber? Uber connects riders, restaurants, couriers, and drivers through one mobility platform for trips, meals, payments, and support.  
Why use the uber app? The uber app organizes uber ride booking, uber eats ordering, uber delivery tracking, saved places, receipts, and account controls.  
Who uses it? Riders, travelers, commuters, restaurants, couriers, companies using uber business, and drivers managing work with uber driver tools.  
Does it cover more than rides? Yes, Uber includes uber taxi options, uber car requests, uber black trips, uber one benefits, and food ordering through uber eats.

## Personal Mobility Guide

For everyday riders, the uber app is most useful when profiles, payment methods, saved places, and notification preferences are configured before a trip is needed. A user can compare uber taxi or uber car options, check estimated arrival times, select a pickup point, and follow the driver on the map. After the trip, receipts and route history remain available for review.

Food ordering works similarly. Uber eats lets users browse nearby restaurants, apply eligible offers, confirm delivery details, and watch uber delivery progress from preparation to arrival. People who order often may evaluate uber one for membership benefits, while occasional users may rely on simple checkout and saved addresses.

Drivers and couriers use uber driver features to accept work, navigate to pickup points, track earnings, and manage account documents. Riders, couriers, and customers all benefit from clear status messages, accurate locations, and accessible uber support when something needs correction.

## Help and Fix Paths

Pickup looks wrong? Adjust the pin in the uber app before confirming the uber ride or message the driver with a clear landmark.  
Order delayed? Check uber eats status, review courier movement, and contact uber support if the uber delivery window changes unexpectedly.  
Receipt missing? Open trip history, choose the uber car or uber taxi trip, and resend the receipt to the account email.  
Membership unclear? Review uber one benefits, renewal timing, and eligible orders before relying on discounts.  
Driver issue? Use uber driver account prompts, document uploads, and help topics to resolve access, navigation, or earnings questions.
